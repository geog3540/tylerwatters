Population Density

Analyzing the population density of New Jersey at the county level. Data was taken from the New Jerser Environmental Protection Agency GIS Division. Data is based off of 2010 census information. Looking at the clustering of high density zones in Northeast Jersey and Southwest Jersey, we have to take a small step back and realize these zones are connected to a larger metropolis. Northeast is connected directly to the Manhatten area and Southwest is right next to Philidelphia. New Jersey itself does not have any of these big name, easy to recognize land marks but it does benefit from being neighbors with a couple.
